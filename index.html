Why a cozy corner?

The name of the project was coined back in 2021.

But why"Cozy Corner"? A cozy corner symbolizes calmness and

patience. On the site, each visitor will find a place where he will be comfortable.

What is the cozy corner for?

You probably had something similar: you are sitting at your

computer, and you have open (discord, telegram, YouTube, Steam,

online games, etc.) on it. And of course, it’s not convenient

for you to use them like that, and who is the husband of all

these applications/games/sites with different themes. For this,

a cozy corner has been created. Cozy Corner has many functions,

games and more.
